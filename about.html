About Us

Direct Action River Falls is a small group of political activists focused on ending hate locally and worldwide and fighting for humanity's survival. Our goal is to create a world inclusive of everyone and without hate. In the past several years since Donald Trump became president, the amount of hate crimes has be steadily increasing, especially in counties that held a Trump rally. Because of this increase in hate, there must be opposition to this hate. We are a part of that opposition. We cannot stand by and allow the extreme right to create more victims. The solution we are using to oppose hate is direct action. Direct action allows us to oppose local hate groups promptly and without complication. Join the fight against hate today and help your everyone who is struggling against hate.
