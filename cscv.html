Citizens for the Saint Croix Valley

The group knows as the Citizens for the Saint Croix Valley is a local association considered a hate group by the Southern Poverty Law Center formed in the Saint Croix valley area. This group is actively anti-islam, as stated on their website and have a history of xenophobic "activism" that includes fighting against asylum for refugees, harrassing local Muslims, and ripping down gay pride flags, as well as promoting conspiracy theories involving Sharia Law, human sex trafficking, and communism. They are also vocal against LGBTQ+ people, promoting the idea that schools are "forcing" children to become LGBTQ+. They link to a website that calls for banning books that have gender, sexual, and romantic minorities (GRSM) in them and advocates other censorship under the guise of concern for "American Values." This hate group must be stopped from gaining momentum and promoting fearful and bigoted views as soon as possible. Join the fight today and help stop hate from spreading.
