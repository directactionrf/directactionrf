Join the Fight

Everyone can help fight against hate. It can be as basic as letting marginalized people know they are welcome or as extreme as staging a large-scale protest against hate groups. Our main focus is on local hate groups in the area near the Twin Cities and Western Wisconsin and our group is not as large as others, so our goals are to inform local citizens about hate groups in the area and deplatform the hate groups so they can no longer spread. To get involved in Direct Action River Falls, email us or check out some other local groups.
